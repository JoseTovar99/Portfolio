Haunted Woods (VR)

This project was one of my earliest projects and what sparked my interest in 3D Modeling. I had a fun time being able to create a haunted scenery, mainly due to my love for ghosts and horro movies. The model was created in Blender and was then transferred to Sketchfab to show the VR effect of the project. In VR mode, the viewer can experience themselves inside of the car with underground fingers engulfing them.
